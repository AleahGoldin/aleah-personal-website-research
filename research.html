Aleah Goldin

Designs research

Aleah is an expert at understanding the big picture and designing research studies that meet consumer and client needs. She has experience setting the research strategy for:

  • Surveys
  • Interviews
  • Focus groups
  • Photovoice
  • Usability testing

Her research, which has been funded by both the State Department and United States Embassy in Mongolia, has won academic awards. Currently, she is employed at CommunicateHealth in the research department and works on federal contracts with rapid-turnarounds.

Collects data

A natural moderator, Aleah conducts studies for a wide range of topics. She uses communication, anthropology, and human-centered design techniques to build rapport and get the most out of sessions. She even uses some innovative methods to collect data and create compelling narratives.

Synthesizes Data

A superhero at synthesizing data, Aleah holistically looks at the data and analyzes it. For large studies, she uses the software platform, NVivo. She has lead trainings on coding transcripts and running complex queries for undergraduate students and departments.

Develops Recommendations

Aleah thrives at developing actionable recommendations that meet both consumer and client needs. Collaboratively, she works with writers and designers on her teams to develop recommendations that are within scope and maintainable. Her goal is to leverage the research to help produce tools and campaigns that exceed expectations.

Writes first-class reports

Aleah is and always has been a writer. Her favorite type of writing is summary memos and topline reports. She has a keen eye for nuance and produces reports in a variety of mediums.
